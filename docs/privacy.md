# Privacy Policy

*Version of 7 July 2026. Preliminary version — the operator's full legal details will be added
once company registration is complete.*

## Who processes your data

The data controller is the owner of the MYXON site and platform. Full legal details will be
published once registration is complete; for any question about your data, please reach us via the
contact form on this site.

## What we collect

**Via the contact form** (provided voluntarily): name, email address, company (optional), role
(optional) and your message.

**Automatically:** IP address — used **solely** to protect the form from spam and automated abuse,
never for profiling.

The site uses **no tracking cookies and no web analytics**. Appearance settings are stored only
locally in your browser.

## Why

To review your enquiry and respond (including dealership and integration matters), and to protect
the service from abuse.

## Legal basis

Processing is based on **your consent**, given by ticking the consent box when submitting the form
(GDPR Art. 6(1)(a); for Russia — Federal Law 152-FZ).

## Sharing

We do **not** share your data with third parties. Processing runs on our own self-hosted
infrastructure. Personal data of Russian citizens is stored on servers located in Russia.

## Retention

Data is kept until your enquiry is resolved, no longer than 12 months from the last interaction, or
until you withdraw consent — whichever is earlier.

## Your rights

You may request access, rectification or erasure of your data, and **withdraw consent** at any time
via the contact form. You may also lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Russia —
Roskomnadzor; EU — your national data protection authority).

## Changes

The current version of this Policy is always available on this page, with its date.
