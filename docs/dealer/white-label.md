# White-Label & Branded Access

Run the portal under **your own brand**. Your customers see your logo, your colours, and your
name — not MYXON. Ideal for integrators, resellers, and OEM manufacturers who want a seamless
experience for their end customers.

> **Screenshot:** *Login page with a partner logo, custom accent colour, and tagline — served from a dedicated subdomain*

## What you can customise

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Logo** | Your logo on the login page and in the header |
| **Brand colours** | Accent colour for buttons and links, plus the login background |
| **Name & tagline** | Your company name and a caption under the logo |
| **Favicon** | The browser tab icon |
| **Your own address** | A dedicated subdomain such as `yourbrand.<domain>` |

Any field left empty stays in the default MYXON style — customise as much or as little as you like.

## How it looks to your customers

Your customer opens your address (for example `yourbrand.<domain>`) and immediately sees your
logo and colours — before they even sign in. Sign-in itself works as usual: by password, or —
when configured — through a single corporate sign-on button.

::: info No extra apps
The branded portal runs entirely in the browser. Your customers don't install anything.
:::

## Corporate sign-on (SSO) for enterprise customers

If your customer is a large enterprise with a corporate identity system (Active Directory,
Azure/Entra, Keycloak, ADFS), sign-in can be tied to it:

- employees sign in with their **existing work account** — no separate MYXON password;
- with seamless sign-on enabled on the enterprise side, access can be **fully automatic** —
  no login prompt at all, right after the user is signed in to Windows.

::: tip Zero-click access
When the enterprise identity provider supports desktop single sign-on, opening the branded
address logs the user straight in — no clicks, no typing.
:::

## How to request it

Branding and corporate sign-on are set up by the **MYXON platform team** on request. Prepare:

1. your logo and favicon (SVG or PNG);
2. your brand colours (hex codes);
3. the name and tagline you want;
4. your preferred subdomain;
5. for SSO — a contact in your customer's IT/security team to establish trust with their identity system.

→ [Contact us](/contact) to enable white-label for your account.
