# Invite Customers

Before a customer can log in to the MYXON platform and claim their devices, they need an account. Dealers create customer accounts using **invite links** — no need to set a password for the customer.

## Create an invite

1. Open **Dealer Portal → Customers** tab
2. Enter the customer's **email address**
3. Enter the **company or customer name** (shown in their account)
4. Click **Generate Invite Link**

> **Screenshot:** *"Invite New Customer" form with email field, company name field, and "Generate Invite Link" button. Helper text: "Send an invite link to the customer. They will create their own account and activate their devices independently."*

## Send the invite

After creation, the invite URL appears:

> **Screenshot:** *Invite result panel (green background): "Invite created for customer@company.nl". URL displayed in monospace box. "Copy Link" and "Open in Mail" buttons. Expiry date below.*

You can:
- **Copy Link** — paste into any messaging channel (email, WhatsApp, Teams)
- **Open in Mail** — opens your default mail client with a pre-filled email

The link is valid for **7 days**. If it expires before the customer registers, create a new invite.

## Customer registration

When the customer opens the invite link, they see a registration form with their email pre-filled. They choose a password and complete registration. Their account is automatically linked to your dealer tenant so you can see their devices.

::: tip Multiple users per customer
You can create multiple invites for the same customer organization — for example, one for the farm owner and one for their technician. Each gets their own login.
:::

## After registration

Once the customer registers, they can:
1. Scan the QR code on their device to claim it
2. See the device in their dashboard
3. Open remote access sessions

They manage their own users, sites, and access policies independently — you as the dealer never see their operational data.
